BTS (방탄소년단) Profile

Background Info:

BTS is a 7-member group under the company Big Hit Entertainment. Over the course of their 7-years career, the group has managed to become the world’s biggest K-Pop group. They continue to break records and make millions of dollars (billions in the Korean currency, won) of profit from concerts, merchandising, and album sales.

A fact that’s not as well-known about BTS is the level of creative control they’re often given over their own music. While they aren't entirely self-producing like groups Seventeen or Stray Kids, members RM and Suga do an impressive amount of the producing, composing, and lyrical writing throughout the group’s discography. RM, BTS’s leader, has the 4th highest number of copyrighted songs in all of K-Pop, with main rapper Suga at a respectable 9th place, as seen in this Allkpop article.

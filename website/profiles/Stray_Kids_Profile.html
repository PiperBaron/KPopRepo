Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈) Profile

Background Info:

Stray Kids is an 8-member group under JYP Entertainment and were formed through a survival-type competition show of the same name. Felix and Lee Know were almost voted off, but were given a second chance and rejoined the group by the finale. Member Woojin left the group in October of 2019.

The group officially debuted with their mini album I am NOT, though they had released the single Hellavtor, the music video for which had been dropped prior to the show’s airing, and the album Mixtape before debut.

There are three subunits that focus on different aspects of their music and performance: rapping (3RACHA), dancing (Dance Racha), and singing (Vocal Racha). 3RACHA (whose naming scheme obviously influenced the other two units) was the first subunit formed and is responsible for the writing, composing, and producing for most of Stray Kids’ music

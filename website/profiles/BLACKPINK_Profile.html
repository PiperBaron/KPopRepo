BLACKPINK (블랙핑크) Profile

Background Info:

BLACKPINK is a 4-member group under YG Entertainment. They debuted in 2016 with their debut single album Square One, which contains the tracks Boombayah and Whistle, both considered their “debut songs.” The tracks charted number 1 and 2 respectively on Billboard’s World Digital Songs chart.

The group has a dual-theme, similar to another well-known group Red Velvet, using the colors black and pink to contrast different ideas and themes. To quote an article from the K-Pop news site Soompi, “The meaning of Black Pink aims to contradict the common perception of the color pink. Pink is commonly used to portray prettiness, but Black Pink actually means to say that ‘Pretty isn’t everything.’ It also symbolizes that they are a team that encompasses not only beauty, but also great talent.”

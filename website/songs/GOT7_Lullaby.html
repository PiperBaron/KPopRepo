Lullaby (럴러바이) Lyrics and Translation

Background Info:

“Lullaby” is the title track of GOT7’s third studio album “Present: YOU”. The song is filled with groovy verses with a fairly heavy bass in the chorus, according to Genius.

It’s the group’s only track that’s available in four different languages: Korean, English, Chinese, and Spanish. The group said, in a press conference, that: “[a]fter meeting our fans all over the world through our world tour, we have been wondering how we could communicate with them more directly, and we thought singing in their own language will do it.”

Song Meaning:

Lullaby is a sweet love song centering around the themes of sleep and dreams. The group is saying that their lover’s voice is literally like a lullaby, lulling them to sleep -- they reference “eyelids getting heavy” and “drifting in and out of slumber.” Once asleep, they don’t want to wake up, they want to stay in this perfect bubble with the girl they love forever, just listening to the sound of her voice.

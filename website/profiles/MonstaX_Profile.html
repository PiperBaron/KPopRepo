Monsta X (몬스타엑스) Profile

Background Info:

Monsta X is a 6-member boy group from Starship Entertainment. They were formed through a survival show named No.Mercy. They debuted as 7 members with the EP Trespass on May 14, 2015, though member Wonho left the group in October of 2019.

The group is known for having a larger following outside of South Korea, finding a large audience in the West, often argued due to their sound and styling. In 2019 the group guest-starred as themselves in an America cartoon called We Bare Bears, which gained them even more interest within the U.S. They released their full-English album All About Luv in early 2020 to great success for the group, with the song Who Do U Love? Hitting number 5 on the Billboard 100.

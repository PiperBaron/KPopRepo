Ridin’ Lyrics and Translation

Background Info:

Ridin' is the title track from NCT Dream’s newest album Reload. It's an urban-trap song, a sound that's rather unique to the group with a more overwhelmingly youthful pop sound.

According to Genius: "In an interview with TV Report (originally in Korean), the members have described it as a song “full of heat” that has a “hip charm and strong energy” and is a good song to listen to while driving."

Song Meaning:

Ridin’ is a high-energy song that conveys a sense of achievement and a desire to “break the boundary.” NCT Dream is a very youthful group with mostly cute and young concepts, and it seems that with this song, they’ve moved on from their past and want to declare their maturity.

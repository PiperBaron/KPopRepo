Simon Says Lyrics and Translation

Background Info:

Simon Says is the title track off of NCT 127’s first full album Regular-Irregular. It’s “an urban hip-hop song with heavy bass riff and sharp synth sounds,” the dark and unique sound of the music fitting well with the message of criticizing society and breaking free from the shackles that culture puts on the people within it.

Song Meaning:

The song and video overall seems to criticise the way society forces people to wear masks in order to fit alongside everyone else, and how cultures create arbitrary rules we’re all expected to follow without question. NCT 127 express how they’re called "vibe killers" for breaking these rules and refusing to measure themselves with the standards set for them. Overall, Simon Says communicates a message of individuality and rebellion against an oppressive over-bearing culture.

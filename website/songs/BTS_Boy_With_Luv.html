Boy With Luv (작은 것들을 위한 시) Lyrics and Translation

Background Info:

Boy With Luv is the title track off of the mini album Map of the Soul: Persona.

BTS collaborated on this song with singer/song-wrtier Halsey, with whom the group has become fast friends. The song broke several records when it was released, both in the US market and internationally, including fastest video to hit 100 million views. The only song s that has been able to beat Boy With Luv’s impressive feats is BTS’s newest single, Dynamite.

Song Meaning:

Boy With Luv is a very sweet kind of song that focuses on the strength of love and how it’s one of the strongest forces in one’s life. The whole song is full of loving references to the group’s past songs and to their fanbase; one big reference is the title and chorus of the song. One of BTS’s most popular songs in their early days was Boy in Luv, which was about unrequited love and changing oneself to fit another person’s needs. Now, in the present, BTS is signalling to their fans and the world at large that they’ve grown, no longer willing to compromise on themselves and instead finding love and inspiration from within.

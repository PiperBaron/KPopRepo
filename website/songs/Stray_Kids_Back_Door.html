Back Door Lyrics and Translation

Background Info:

Back Door was released as the title track off of Stray Kids' first repackaged album, IN生.

The track was co-composed by members of the group's own subunit 3RACHA (Han, Changbin, and leader Bang Chan), and was apparently praised for its production by JYP Entertainment’s J.Y. Park himself.

Song Meaning:

Back Door and its lyrics seem to invite the listener into a private sector, where only “authorized personnel” are allowed -- Stray Kids are beckoning fans and non-fans alike to join them in their new-found success. Off of the back of the wildly popular God’s Menu, the title-track of their previous full album GO生, Stray Kids are flexing their fame and presenting an invitation into their fandom to anyone with the privilege to listen.
